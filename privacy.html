Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-15

This Privacy Policy explains how OpenClaw TikTok Auto Farm ("Service", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account and OAuth Information

  • TikTok OAuth tokens (access token, refresh token),
  • TikTok account identifier(s) returned by authorized APIs.

b) Content and Metadata

  • video title/caption/hashtags,
  • generated scripts and content metadata,
  • posting status and timestamps.

c) Technical Data

  • logs needed for troubleshooting and monitoring,
  • basic usage diagnostics.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • authenticate and maintain your connection to TikTok APIs,
  • publish content you request or schedule,
  • track pipeline status and posting results,
  • improve reliability and security of the Service.

3. Legal Basis / Permission

For connected platform actions, data is processed based on your explicit authorization via OAuth and your direct use of the Service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.
We may share data only when necessary to operate integrations (e.g., TikTok API requests you authorize) or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, logs and debugging, and legal or security requirements.

You can request deletion of stored integration data (including tokens), subject to legal obligations.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect stored data, including restricted access and secure credential handling.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • revoke TikTok authorization at any time from your TikTok settings,
  • request access/update/deletion of your stored data,
  • stop using the Service at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. International Transfers

If applicable, your data may be processed in jurisdictions different from your own, with appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
